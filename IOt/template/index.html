Development Approach

Being able to track the real quantity of water contamination is the first step in controlling it. The challenge with water pollution monitoring is the labor-intensive process of repeatedly carrying a boat through a lake or reservoir to check for pollutants. Therefore, we have developed a method for quickly assessing the water quality of large bodies of water. The RC water pollution monitor boat enables both recording and online transmission of data regarding water quality.
